## Overview
Biophilic reconnects digital with natural patterns: dappled light, organic asymmetry, flowing forms. Elements feel grown, not built.

## Color System
Earth and plant pigments — low contrast, multiple tonal zones for harmony.

## Typography
Alegreya (friendly serif) for headlines; Lora (moderate slab) for body. Generous height (1.75) and x-height.

## Shapes
Asymmetric border-radius values; organic, hand-formed feel.

## Layout
Loose grid with flowing guides; elements drift by 4-8px to mimic craft variance.

## Textures
Noise grain; soft gradients; dappled light follows mouse.

## Components
Buttons: leaf green fill with soft shadow; lift gently on hover. Cards: inner highlight top-left like wet surface.

## Motion
Slow (`0.6s`), ease-out. Scale-up from 0.97 on entry.

## Do's and Don'ts
- Use organic border-radius values
- Apply grain/noise
- Allow slight off-grid
- Favor rounded rectangles
- Warm, earth-derived colors only
- Moderate saturation
- Don't use pure white/black
- Don't use pixxel fonts
- Don't use hard edges
