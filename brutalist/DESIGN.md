## Overview
Brutalism rejects softness. The aesthetic embraces raw, unpolished, aggressive presentation — borrowed from mid-20th century brutalist architecture: honest materials, massive scale, no ornamental pretense. Beauty emerges from contrast and rhythm alone.

## Color System
High-contrast acid against deep black. Pure, saturated, often jarring.
- `--bg`: `#111111` — deep charcoal, the void
- `--acid`: `#ccff00` — neon lime, the primary action
- `--red`, `--blue`, `--highlight` — emphasize with vibration

## Typography
Massive scale. `h1` is 6rem and dominates the viewport. **Archivo Black** condenses like a poster. Background is **Space Mono** — terminal, raw.

## Elevation
Elevation simulated with hard shadows (4px solid black offsets, no blur). Elements appear cut from sheet steel.

## Shapes
Borders are 2–4px black; radius max 4px, often 0. The aesthetic is rectilinear and machine-made.

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do use huge type that overflows
- Do keep the black or white outline on everything
- Do layer 8px offsets
- Do never soften edges
- Don't use soft shadows or opacity
- Don't align to a perfect grid — offset by multiples of 8px for tension
