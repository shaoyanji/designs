## Overview
Mid-century commercial art revived: boldness, saturated colors, hard shadows. Playful without childish. Warm background; colors pop like candy.

## Color System
Warm paper tone `#fdf6e3`. Orange is primary action; teal, lavender, pink, mustard, lime are equally weighted.

### Borders
All borders are solid black at 2–4px. No curves, no rounded-caps.

## Elevation
Buttons: 4px hard black shadow; hover compresses shadow as if pressed.

Cards: 6px solid black shadow with no blur.

## Typography
Massive typographic contrast. h1 at 3rem, body at 1rem. Vollkorn serif headlines; Archivo geometric for subheads; Public Sans neutral body.

## Layout
Asymmetric but feels balanced. Large colored blocks counterpoint headlines.

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do use black borders on every surface
- Do paper background warm, never white
- Do let colors clash (within the palette)
- Do use hard shadows only – no blur
- Don't use thin lines (minimum 2px)
- Don't use cool colors like cyan/steel blue
- Do vary font sizes dramatically
- Don't omit a border — everything has a black edge
