## Overview
Memphis-Milano (1981) rejected good taste, replaced it with deliberate clash. Jungle colors, wild geometry, childish scribbles rendered with serious typography — playfulness as a valid goal.

## Color System
Dopamine palette of saturated joy against warm cream (`#fff9f0`). All colors fully saturated.

## Forms
Fragmented: triangles, circles, squiggles. 3px black borders everywhere. Shadow solid black offset 4px — sticker-like quality.

## Layout
Asymmetric collage; elements overlap, layered with -2 to +5 degree rotations, feeling hand-placed.

## Typography
Fonts with character: Architects Daughter (handwritten, rough), Comic Neue (ironic comic), Nunito (round friendly).

## Components
Buttons slight chew (`rotate(-1deg)`), solid black shadow. Card border 3px; squiggle divider (`border-bottom: 4px dashed teal`). Triangle badges — yellow, tilted.

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do rotate elements -2 to +5 degrees
- Do use thick black borders on everything
- Do layer bright colors to clash deliberately
- Do offset by 4-8px to simulate hand placement
- Do include dashed/squiggle lines
- Do use checkerboard patterns (2x2 grids)
- Don't smooth anything; embrace raw
- Don't remove borders
- Don't worry about alignment perfection
