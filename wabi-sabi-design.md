## Overview
Wabi-sabi finds beauty in imperfection. Design as aged, textured, porous. Light falls across surfaces as if morning on paper.

## Color System
All colors are desaturated and warm — pigment has aged 20 years.
Text is charcoal `#3d3a32`, never pure black.

## Typography
Serifs with ink traps. Cormorant Garamond for headlines; Lora for body. Airy leading (1.7). Text flows like a brush.

## Layout
Loose grid; margins vary by 4–8px intentionally. Measures vary slightly to suggest organic growth.

## Textures
Paper grain: subtle noise. Stain marks: feathered ellipses with blur, opacity 0.08. Cards use a vignette mask.

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do vary spacing slightly
- Do use stains and grain overlays
- Do use serif faces
- Do allow elements to be slightly off-grid
- Don't use pure black
- Don't use hard borders
- Don't use perfect circles or squares
- Don't use uppercase exclusively
