## Overview
Fresco — paint on wet plaster — binds color to the wall itself. UI appears hand-applied, weathered, and textured. Light falls across surfaces as if from a candle.

## Color System
Pigments from the tube: sienna, ochre, terra, verdigris, cinnabar, ultramarine, gold-leaf. None are pure RGB — each bears earth's unevenness.

## Typography
Letterpress feel — not screen. Cormorant Garamond for its engraved plate contrast; Source Serif 4 for comfortable reading.

## Surface & Material
Simulate plaster with subtle noise grain or a linear gradient for trowel marks. Inner highlight on top-left mimics sunlight on rough surface.

## Borders
Thin (`1px`) warm gray, never pure black.

## Components
Buttons: pigment blocks with inner shadow. Cards: vignette mask fades edges.

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do treat every element as painted
- Do layer surfaces with transparent overlays
- Do use warm grays
- Do include grain on all backgrounds
- Do use accent only where emphasis matters
- Do classical proportions — golden ratio where possible
- Don't use pure white or black
- Don't oversaturate
- Don't perfect alignment — organic
